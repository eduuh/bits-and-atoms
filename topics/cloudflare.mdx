Cloudflare provides a powerful suite of tools for deploying and scaling web applications. From static site hosting with Pages to serverless compute with Workers, there's a lot to explore.

## Why Cloudflare?

- **Global CDN**: Your content is served from data centers worldwide
- **Zero Cold Starts**: Workers run at the edge with instant response times
- **Generous Free Tier**: Perfect for personal projects and small apps
- **Integrated Security**: DDoS protection, WAF, and SSL out of the box
